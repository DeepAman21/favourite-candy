Smarties : My Favourite Candy

Fun-Facts About Smarties

Smarties® have long been a favorite of ours! As a kid, we used them as “medicine” when we played hospitals and doctors. As a parent, I use them as a sweet reward for a job well done!

  • Rowntree's of York, England, have been making "Chocolate Beans" since at least 1882.
  • Rowntree's was forced to drop the words "chocolate beans" in 1937 due to trading standards requirements so adopted the "Milk Chocolate in a Crisp Sugar Shell". Later, the sweet was rebranded as "Smarties".
  • Smarties come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, yellow, green, purple, and pink.
  • Over the last 25 years,[as of?] Nestlé and Rowntree's have manufactured five billion Smarties lids. Some lids are very rare and are now regarded as collectors' items
  • Smarties are also sold in the form of chocolate bars and eggs with fragments of Smarties in them, and chocolate-and-vanilla ice cream with Smarties pieces in it known as Smarties Fusion.

Nutritional Information

  • A roll of Smarties® has only 25 calories!
  • Smarties® are gluten free. In fact, their entire line is gluten free.
  • All Smarties® products are vegetarian and vegan.
